The Weekend

Two days. Three pastors. One vision.

A weekend gathering for the whole family — opening Friday evening, closing Saturday afternoon — devoted to the Bible's vision of man, woman, and home in a time of confusion.

"The speakers will be Peter Bringe, Brian De Jong, and Mike Myers — three pastors of the Orthodox Presbyterian Church."

The Speakers

Three voices from the OPC.

Faithful ministers from Missouri, Wisconsin, and Georgia, each opening the Scriptures to the questions families face today.

01

Peter Bringe

Covenant Family OPC · Wentzville, MO

Rev. Bringe has lived most of his life in St. Charles County and joined Covenant Family Church with his family at twelve. BA in Bible and Theology from Whitefield College; MDiv from Covenant Theological Seminary. Ordained on October 9, 2018. He and his wife Melody have seven children. On the side, he enjoys folk music, history, and the outdoors.

02

Brian De Jong

Grace OPC · Sheboygan, WI

Born in Bozeman, Montana in 1963. BA in Cinema and Photography (SIU-C); MDiv from Reformed Theological Seminary. Ordained 1993 (PCA) for campus ministry at the University of Florida; planted a church in Marion, IL; pastored Covenant OPC in Brighton, MI. Since 2005 he has been pastor of Grace OPC in Sheboygan. He and DeLou have six children and eight grandchildren.

03

Mike Myers

Heritage OPC · Royston, GA

Graduated from Greenville Presbyterian Theological Seminary in May 2013 and called as organizing pastor of Heritage Presbyterian Church (OPC). Ordained on December 6, 2013. When the church particularized in September 2017, Heritage called him to stay on as minister. He and his wife Katy have eight children.
